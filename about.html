About the Company

ANTINA MARINE is dedicated to provide the shipping industry with high quality, reliable, cost effective and professional consultancy and solutions in the fields of Engineering, Naval Architecture, Site supervision, Project Management and Training in the Oil and Gas sectors. Our Main Goal is to build a company doing Quality work and being a trustworthy partner to our Customers providing the optimal solutions keeping Safety as our Highest priority

We have extensive experience in the operation, maintenance and Conversion of tankers, Bulk Carriers, Shuttles, LPG and LNG vessels.

Please enquire about our services by contacting any of our given contact numbers.

Our Team

Anoop Jacob

Founder / Director Operations

Anoop is the Founder / Director of this company. He is a Marine Engineer and alumni of the Marine Engineering College in Calcutta. He served his time in Great Eastern, World Wide, Mobil and Tanker Pacific Singapore. After serving as Chief Engineer in Tanker Pacific he was called ashore to become a Superintendent in the Head office in Singapore in 2001. During this time he worked extensively on Crude Oil tankers and Product Carriers and did several repairs, conversions and  drydockings all over the world. After working as a Superintendent in Tanker Pacific for 7 years he joined Teekay Singapore in 2007 as a Vessel Manager taking care of the Operation, Running and Maintenance of Aframax, Suezmax and Product tankers. During the 12 years spent in Teekay until the end of 2018 he has worn several hats both in the Oil as well as Gas sectors. This includes being owners representative overlooking extensive structutal repairs on a Fleet of Shuttle tankers operated by Petrobras Brazil, opening and spearheading the LNG technical office in Mumbai and working extensively on several FSRU based LNG projects. He has also inspected a number of LNG terminals and it was during his role as a Project Development Manager in Teekay Gas that the Company won its first contract to build and operate an LNG import terminal.

In his spare time Anoop loves listening to jazz and is a keen Golfer.

Craig Jackson

Co Founder / Director Ports and Terminals

Craig went to sea as a cadet with Ocean Fleets on General Cargo and Ro Ro vessels. After qualifying he joined Bibby Line and later Mid East Ship Management serving on Oil, Chemical and Gas tankers.

After sailing as a Master he joined Golar LNG London as a Superintendent and later as owners representative in North America.In 2005 he moved to Vancouver and joined Teekay Shipping to support the development of the LNG Group. During his time in Teekay he served in a number of roles including Manager Gas Operations and Manager Health, Safety and Quality Assurance before being seconded to the Society of International Gas tankers and Terminal operators (SIGGTO) as a Technical Adviser.

On his return to Teekay he worked with the Business Development Team as Director LNG Ports and Terminals to support Teekays drive into LNG import terminals. Whilst he was in this role Teekay won its first contract to build and operate an LNG import terminal.

In his spare time Craig loves skiing down the slopes of Whistler, travelling and going on camping trips in his Caravan.

Cameron Munro

Director / Structural Integrity

Cameron is our Head of Structural Integrity and our "Go to Man" for anything to do with Structural modifications or Naval Architecture

He started as shipright with Yarrow ShipBuilders and has had a long distinguished career doing several jobs with Top Notch companies. He started as a Hull Inspector with BP Shipping where he supervised the construction of the 1st ever Double Hull tanker.He moved on to being Structural Superintendent for 10 years with Unicom Ship Management doing several docking and Conversion Projects.He moved to Teekay as Hull Superintendent in 2006 working out of Glasgow office for 7 years doing docking supervision, pre docking inspections and also worked on FSO’s and FPSO’s. He has also worked in Norway for Dolphin Drilling working on their Drilling Rigs and Drill ships

Cameron is a marathoner and a fitness fanatic. In his spare time you will find him jogging, cycling, swimming or reading.
