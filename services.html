LNG & LPG Terminals

We can support you with your Terminal development plans including;
  • Concept evaluation and development
  • Studies and workshops including uptime, HAZID/HAZOP
  • Interfacing with authorities, local communities and other stakeholders
  • FSRU and FSU specifications
  • On jetty and FSRU regasification systems and across jetty and ship to ship transfers
  • Approach, manoeuvring and mooring studies
  • Managing FEED and EPC contractors

Drydocking and Conversion Projects

We can provide Drydock supervision and Project Management for Drydocking Tankers as well as LPG and LNG vessels. This includes preparation of specifications, site attendance and Naval Architecture support for any major Structural modifications

HSEQ and Training

We support the industry’s constant drive for improvement by providing training to ship and shore staff and closing the loop through evaluating the effectiveness of the management system. We can provide;
  • Auditing against the Management system, ISM, ISO, TMSA and SIRE.
  • Managing the follow up and closure of audit findings
  • Training to deliver campaigns or to address weaknesses that may be highlighted in an audit
  • Subjective assessment of the vessels operation and management team
  • Incident Investigation

Shipyard Representation

We represent a number of shipyards in Europe and the Far East.
We can assist you to get docking space and repair berths and work out solutions so that your vessels can complete repairs without delays in the fastest possible time. We can negotiate the best rates from the shipyards and can also assist with site visits when your vessels are in the yard to sort out any issues causing delays or cost overruns to the vessel.

LNG as a Fuel

LNG as a fuel is in its infancy and likely to grow significantly in market share. Whilst the LNG industry has decades of experience using LNG as a fuel it will be some time before the wider shipping industry develops such experience. We can assist you with evaluating the benefits of LNG for your fleet and if appropriate transitioning to the use of LNG as a fuel.
  • Concept and design evaluation including supply chains, safety studies, scheduling and capacity
  • Interfacing with authorities, local communities and other stakeholders
  • Development of procedures and other documents for operations, maintenance and dry docking
  • Training of ships staff to STCW standards (Basic, Advanced and Firefighting)

Spares Stockists

We are a stockist and Traders of new and reconditioned Marine Ship spare parts. We can supply spares at short notice all over the world
